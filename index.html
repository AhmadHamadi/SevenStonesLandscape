Common Questions

Interlock patio costs vary based on size, material choice, and site conditions. In the Hamilton area, a basic patio typically starts around $8,000–$12,000, while larger or more complex projects with premium stone can reach $25,000+. We provide detailed, itemized quotes so you know exactly what you're paying for. Contact us for a free on-site estimate.

Yes. We stand behind our craftsmanship. All hardscaping installations include a warranty on workmanship. Material warranties vary by manufacturer and product. We'll walk you through the specific warranty terms during your consultation and include them in your written contract.

Project timelines depend on scope and complexity. A standard patio installation typically takes 3–7 days. Larger projects involving retaining walls, grading, and full landscape design may take 2–4 weeks. We provide a clear project timeline in your quote and keep you updated throughout.

In most cases, residential patios and pathways don't require permits in Hamilton. However, retaining walls over a certain height and significant grading work may require permits or neighbour notifications. We handle this research for you and advise on any requirements during your consultation.

Spring through fall (April–November) is ideal for most outdoor work in Hamilton. Sod installs best in spring or early fall. Hardscaping can be done throughout the season. We recommend booking early — our schedule fills quickly in spring. Contact us any time of year to get on the calendar.

Absolutely. Seven Stones Landscape is fully insured with liability coverage and WSIB (Workplace Safety and Insurance Board) coverage for all workers on your property. We're happy to provide proof of insurance before any project begins.

Yes — always. We offer free, no-obligation on-site consultations and written estimates. There's no pressure and no cost to get our expert opinion on your project. Just fill out our form above or give us a call to get started.
