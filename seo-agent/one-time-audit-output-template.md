# One-Time SEO Audit Output Template

Use this template for the final deliverable after a one-time audit. The goal is a clear, prioritized implementation plan, not a generic SEO checklist.

## 1. Direct Answer

Answer the user's core question first.

Examples:

- Why are we last on Google Maps?
- Why are our SEO pages ranking low?
- Why is this competitor beating us?
- What should we fix first?
- What is blocking indexability?

- Medium: useful improvement, but not blocking.

## 16. AI Search Visibility

| Page/Entity | Crawlability Gap | Extractability Gap | Entity/Trust Gap | Fix |
|---|---|---|---|---|

Focus on clear, factual, crawlable, helpful content. Do not claim guaranteed AI Overview visibility.

Include:

- Google AI eligibility: indexable, snippet-eligible, helpful content, textual content.
- OpenAI crawler controls: OAI-SearchBot, GPTBot, ChatGPT-User.
- Snippet controls: `nosnippet`, `data-nosnippet`, `max-snippet`.
- Main content extractability.
- Entity clarity and structured data alignment.

## 18. 30/60/90 Day Plan

- Top content/cannibalization fixes
- Quick technical wins

### Days 31-60

- Content rewrites and new priority pages
- Competitor gap projects
- Schema/entity improvements
- Citations and local authority

### Days 61-90

- Larger content/authority campaigns
- Programmatic/location page expansion if justified
- Conversion improvements
- Measurement review and second optimization pass

## 20. Final QA Checklist

- Recommendations are evidence-backed.
- Simple changes are separated from larger strategic work.
- Website fixes are separated from GBP fixes.
- GSC exclusions are classified as intentional vs harmful.
- Competitor claims are based on extracted evidence.
- No fake reviews, fake schema, keyword-stuffed names, doorway pages, or link schemes.
- The plan is prioritized by impact and effort.
- Ranking guarantees are not promised.
