Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about walkway installation in Hamilton, Burlington and Oakville.

For a typical 3 to 4 foot wide front walkway in Hamilton, Burlington, or Oakville, 2026 pricing usually runs about $35 to $60 per linear foot for concrete-paver interlock and $70 to $120 per linear foot for Wiarton flagstone or Indiana limestone. Prices include excavation, 6 to 8 inches of 3/4-clear crushed stone base, geotextile fabric, polymeric sand, and edge restraint. Get a free online estimate with 24-hour response.
For a primary front walkway in Ontario homes, we recommend a minimum 4 feet wide so two adults can walk side by side, which also aligns with OBC accessibility norms. Secondary side paths can be 30 to 36 inches. Wider walkways (5 to 6 feet) feel more welcoming on larger Oakville or Ancaster lots and are easier to snow-blow in winter.
In Hamilton, Dundas, Ancaster, and Stoney Creek where clay subgrade is common, we excavate 10 to 12 inches and install compacted 3/4-clear crushed stone in 2-inch lifts over geotextile fabric. Clay holds water, so the geotextile prevents the base from migrating into the clay over freeze-thaw cycles and keeps the walkway level for decades.
As authorized Unilock and Techo-Bloc installers we regularly build walkways with Unilock Artline, Unilock Beacon Hill Flagstone, Techo-Bloc Borealis, and Techo-Bloc Blu 60. For natural stone we use Wiarton flagstone (Ontario-quarried), Indiana limestone, and large-format granite steppers.
Private residential walkways on your own lot generally do not require a permit in Burlington, Oakville, or Hamilton. However, if the walkway extends into the city road allowance, crosses a boulevard, or involves grading changes over 0.3 metres, a municipal permit is usually required. We handle the paperwork when needed.
A standard 40 to 80 linear foot interlock walkway takes 3 to 5 working days: one day for excavation and base prep, one to two days for base compaction and laying pavers, and a final day for polymeric sand and cleanup. Flagstone walkways take slightly longer due to custom cutting. We carry $5M commercial general liability and full WSIB coverage, and every walkway is backed by our 5-year workmanship warranty.
