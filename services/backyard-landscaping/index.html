Based on projects across Hamilton, Burlington, and Oakville since 2013, the top ROI features are (1) Unilock or Techo-Bloc interlock patios sized for a real dining table plus seating, (2) privacy through cedar fencing or board-formed concrete, (3) drainage corrections and 2% slope away from the house, and (4) a lit walkway connecting deck to fire pit for evening use.
A standard 2,500 sq ft backyard rebuild takes 4 to 7 weeks on-site, split roughly: week 1 demolition and grading; weeks 2-3 retaining walls, drainage, and patio base; week 4 paver installation and edge restraint; week 5 fire pit, pergola, and lighting; weeks 6-7 sod, planting, and final cleanup. Weather and special-order Unilock or Techo-Bloc colours can add time.
