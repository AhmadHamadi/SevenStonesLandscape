Complete Landscaping and Hardscaping Services Across Hamilton, Burlington, and Oakville

Seven Stones Landscape provides full-scope landscaping and hardscaping for homeowners who want long-lasting results, not temporary fixes. Our team plans each project around Ontario freeze-thaw conditions, site drainage, and practical day-to-day use of your yard. Whether you are renovating the front entrance, rebuilding a failing driveway, or planning a complete backyard transformation, we coordinate design, preparation, and installation as one integrated process.

Homeowners in Hamilton, Burlington, Oakville, Ancaster, Dundas, Waterdown, Stoney Creek, Milton, and Mississauga commonly hire us for interlock patios, concrete driveways, retaining walls, sod installation, and grading and drainage correction. We also build walkways, install landscape stone features, and deliver complete backyard landscaping and front yard curb appeal upgrades.

Our process starts with understanding your goals, budget, and site constraints. We then create a plan that prioritizes structural performance first: excavation depth, compaction, base build-up, grade control, and water management. Finish selections come next, including paver patterns, concrete finishes, planting layouts, and lighting placement. This sequence helps reduce costly rework and ensures every element works together.

How We Structure Quotes and Project Phasing

Every quote is broken down into scope items so homeowners can compare options clearly. We identify essential structural work first, then optional upgrades for style and future expansion.
