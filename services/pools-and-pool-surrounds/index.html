For Hamilton, Burlington, and Oakville pools, we recommend textured Unilock Umbriano, Techo-Bloc Blu 60 Smooth, or honed travertine for strong wet-slip resistance and cooler surface temperatures. Ontario pool decks fail when water pools under the surface and freezes. We install a 6-8 inch compacted base of 3/4-clear crushed stone (open-graded) over Mirafi 140N geotextile, grade the deck at 2% minimum away from the pool coping per OBC guidance, and add perimeter trench drains or Quick Drain systems where lawn meets paver. Open-graded bases drain 10x faster than traditional road-base setups, which virtually eliminates heaving through Hamilton-area freeze-thaw cycles.
