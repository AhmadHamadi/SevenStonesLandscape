# Local Services Ads And Policy Guardrails - May 2026

Use this before advising on Local Services Ads, Google Verified, Google Guaranteed, locksmiths, garage doors, contractors, or any home-service category.

## LSA Fundamentals

- Local Services Ads are profile-driven and lead-based. They use business hours, service areas, services offered, phone, reviews, photos, verification, and responsiveness.
- LSAs rank through an auction that considers bid, likely lead result, responsiveness, search context, relevance, ratings/reviews, average response time, photos, verification checks, and other profile-quality signals.
- Google recommends Maximize Leads for optimal LSA performance. Target CPL can add control, but Google notes Maximize Leads is generally recommended.
- Google says Maximize Leads may need about two weeks for results to stabilize and recommends a budget around at least 10 leads per week for optimal results.
- If unwanted leads come in, refine service areas, job types, and categories. Do not rely only on disputes/credits.

## LSA Profile Checklist

- Verified Google Business Profile where required.
- Correct business name, phone, hours, service area, and categories.
- Licenses, insurance, and background checks completed where required.
- Strong review count and rating. Some categories/countries require reviews before ads can go live.
- High-quality real photos: team, vehicles, completed jobs, shop/office, before/after where permitted.
- Messages and/or bookings enabled if the business can respond quickly.
- Staff assigned to answer leads during all advertised hours.

## Google Guarantee / Google Verified Note

- Google documentation states that effective November 7, 2025, the Google Guarantee badge and associated Money Back Guarantee were discontinued in favor of Google Verified badge language in some contexts.
- Do not promise customers reimbursement, guarantee coverage, or badge benefits unless current account/category/country docs confirm the exact badge and terms.

## Restricted Contractor Categories

- Locksmith services and garage door repair services in the United States require advanced verification for Google Ads.
- Locksmith services in Canada require advanced verification.
- Some countries restrict or disallow certain local service ads, including locksmiths and plumbers in specified regions. Always verify by country/category before launch.

## Policy Behavior For Contractors

- Providers and agencies are responsible for employees, contractors, subcontractors, and workers who serve customers.
- Price ranges should not be increased on arrival unless scope or circumstances materially differ from what the customer described.
- Diagnostic charges and trip fees must be disclosed before service.
- Do not pass the LSA lead cost directly to the customer as a line item.
- Provide receipts with company name and services performed.
- Be responsive before, during, and after service. Repeated missed calls or unresolved disputes can harm eligibility/performance.
- Only accept work within license requirements and expertise.
